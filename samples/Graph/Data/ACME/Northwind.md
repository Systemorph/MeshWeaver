# What's Inside

## Analytics Platform

Explore **53 views** organized across 8 categories:

- **Dashboard** - Executive overview with key metrics
- **Sales** - Revenue analysis and category comparisons
- **Orders** - Order patterns and fulfillment tracking
- **Products** - Product performance and trends
- **Customers** - Customer insights and segmentation
- **Employees** - Sales rep performance
- **Suppliers** - Supplier analytics
- **Financial** - Financial metrics and profitability

## Key Features Demonstrated

**Virtual Data Cube**
The `NorthwindDataCube` combines Orders, OrderDetails, and Products into a unified analytics layer for efficient aggregation and flexible filtering.

**Multi-Year Analytics**
Data covers 2024-2025 with year-over-year comparison, growth metrics, trend analysis, and seasonal pattern identification.

**AI-Assisted Exploration**
Use natural language queries to explore data:
- "Show me sales by category for 2025"
- "Who are our top customers?"
- "Compare revenue between 2024 and 2025"
