# Welcome to MeshWeaver

MeshWeaver is your collaborative knowledge platform that brings together documents, approvals, and AI-powered assistance in one place.

## What you can do

- **Organize knowledge** — Create and structure documents, code, and data in a flexible graph hierarchy
- **Collaborate** — Comment on documents, request approvals, and track changes
- **Chat with AI** — Get instant answers, generate content, and automate workflows
- **Stay informed** — Receive notifications for approval requests, decisions, and updates

## Getting started

[Sign in](/login) to access your personal dashboard with recent activity, notifications, and pending actions.

Or try the chat widget below to ask a question — no account required.
