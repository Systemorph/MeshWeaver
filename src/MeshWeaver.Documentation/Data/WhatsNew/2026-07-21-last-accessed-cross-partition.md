# Last accessed now works across all your spaces

The home page's **Last accessed** ordering previously missed anything you opened outside your own
area, so for many users the list came up empty or showed odd entries. It now reflects your real
recent activity: whatever you opened last — documents, threads, courses — regardless of which
space it lives in, most recent first.

Two smaller clean-ups ride along: your own profile node no longer appears in your home catalog or
activity feeds, and the home list reliably stays a tidy first-level index (spaces and your own
top-level items) instead of occasionally dumping deeply nested pages.
